# Merits & Flaws

Merits and Flaws add depth and distinction to your character. **Merits** are advantageous traits — natural talents, cybernetic enhancements, or connections — that cost Freebie Points equal to their listed value. **Flaws** are disadvantages — psychological weaknesses, physical impairments, or social burdens — that grant additional Freebie Points equal to their listed value.

## Rules

- **Net limit:** The total of your Merits minus your Flaws cannot exceed **7 points** at character creation.
- **Multiple purchases:** Many Merits and Flaws can be taken multiple times. When they can, the description will say so.
- **Storyteller approval:** All Merits and Flaws require Storyteller approval. Some may be inappropriate for certain campaigns.
- **In-play acquisition:** Flaws may be acquired during play (through injury, trauma, or story events) without granting Freebie Points. Merits may be purchased with Experience Points after play begins.

## Alien Physiology

The **Alien Physiology** category contains traits that are normally species-specific — they represent biological adaptations that entire species share. These are marked in the detail view as "Species-specific — non-human only."

A human character cannot normally take an Alien Physiology merit or flaw. However, the Storyteller may allow exceptions for characters with cybernetic replacements that mimic these traits, or for unusual origins.

For species templates that include these traits (with their freebie point adjustments already calculated), see the [Species](./species.mdx) page.
