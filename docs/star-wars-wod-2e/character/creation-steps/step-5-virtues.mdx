## Step 5: Allocate Virtues (7 Dots)

**Virtues** represent your character's emotional and ethical relationship with the Force. Three virtues, **7 dots** total:

| Virtue           | Description                                                                                                   |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Conscience**   | Your sense of right and wrong. High: Yoda, Padmé Amidala. Low: Jabba, Darth Maul.                             |
| **Passion**      | Your capacity for strong emotion — love, hate, joy, anger. High: Han Solo, Kylo Ren. Low: Chirrut Îmwe, Yoda. |
| **Self-Control** | Your patience, resolve, and focus. High: R2-D2, Ben Kenobi. Low: Chewbacca, Kylo Ren.                         |

- Each virtue starts with **1 inherent dot** (so you place 4 more, for a total of 7 dots — but the formula above accounts for this).
- The maximum at character creation is **5**.

:::info[Games Without Force Users]
If your campaign has no Force-using characters, you may **ignore Virtues entirely**. Set starting **Willpower** to 6 and starting **Force Points** to 3. Players may raise these with Freebie or Experience points later.
:::

### Derived Stats

Once Virtues are allocated, calculate these derived values automatically:

| Stat                     | Formula                  |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------ |
| **Willpower**            | Passion + Self-Control   |
| **Force Points**         | Self-Control             |
| **Dark Side Resistance** | 5 + Conscience - Passion |

- Raising a Virtue later **does not** retrigger these formulas — they are set at creation.
- Willpower, Force Points, and Dark Side Resistance can each be raised independently with Freebie or Experience points.

See [Virtues & Willpower](../virtues-willpower.mdx) and [Dark Side Resistance](../dark_side_resistance.mdx) for deep dives.

**Example:** _Jax_ — not Force-sensitive — still has Virtues: Conscience 3, Passion 2, Self-Control 3. Derived: Willpower = 5, Force Points = 3, Dark Side Resistance = 6.
