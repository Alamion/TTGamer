## Step 4: Spend Backgrounds (5 Dots)

**Backgrounds** represent the narrative assets your character has accumulated — wealth, allies, reputation, equipment, and status. You have **5 dots** to allocate across any combination of backgrounds.

| Background        | Effect                                                           |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Allies**        | People who will help you without immediate payment               |
| **Contacts**      | Informants and sources of information across the galaxy          |
| **Customization** | A piece of equipment improved beyond stock specifications        |
| **Droid**         | You own an NPC droid (degree depends on dots)                    |
| **Influence**     | Pull with organizations — government, guild, or underworld       |
| **Mentor**        | An experienced teacher who advises you                           |
| **Military Rank** | Formal rank in a military organization (Army or Navy)            |
| **Noble Status**  | Title and social standing from noble birth                       |
| **Reputation**    | You are known — for better or worse — across the galaxy          |
| **Resources**     | Disposable income and material wealth                            |
| **Retainers**     | Loyal followers who carry out your orders                        |
| **Vehicle**       | A personal vehicle reserved for your use (scale depends on dots) |

See [Backgrounds](../backgrounds.mdx) for full descriptions and dot-rank tables.

**Droid Characters:** May use their Resources background to build equipment directly into their chassis at character creation.
