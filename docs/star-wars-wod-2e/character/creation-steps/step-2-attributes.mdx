## Step 2: Allocate Attributes (7 / 5 / 3)

Your character has nine **Attributes** divided into three categories:

| Category     | Attributes                         |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------- |
| **Physical** | Strength, Dexterity, Stamina       |
| **Social**   | Charisma, Manipulation, Appearance |
| **Mental**   | Perception, Intelligence, Wits     |

Rank these categories as **Primary** (7 dots), **Secondary** (5 dots), and **Tertiary** (3 dots). Within each category, distribute the dots across its three Attributes as you see fit.

- Every Attribute starts with **1 inherent dot** — the minimum possible is 1, the maximum at character creation is **5**.
- WEG's species minimums and maximums do not apply. Player characters are not average specimens.

<br />

**Example:** _Jax Vorn_ is a smuggler and pilot first, a fighter second, and a talker third. He assigns **Physical** as Primary (7 dots), **Mental** as Secondary (5 dots), and **Social** as Tertiary (3 dots). His final attributes: Strength 2, Dexterity 4, Stamina 3 | Perception 3, Intelligence 2, Wits 3 | Charisma 2, Manipulation 1, Appearance 2.
