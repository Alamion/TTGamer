## Step 7: Spend Freebie Points (15 Points)

You have **15 Freebie Points** to fine-tune your character. These can raise existing traits or purchase Merits (see [Step 6](./step-6-merits-flaws) for Merits & Flaws).

### Freebie Cost Table

| Trait                    | Cost per dot       | Notes                                                                |
| ------------------------ | ------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Attribute**            | 5 points           | Raises one Attribute by 1 (max 5)                                    |
| **Ability**              | 2 points           | Raises one Ability (Talent/Skill/Knowledge) by 1 (max 3 at creation) |
| **Virtue**               | 2 points           | Raises Conscience, Passion, or Self-Control by 1 (max 5)             |
| **Force Skill**          | 3 points           | Requires the Force Sensitive Merit first                             |
| **Force Power**          | 2 points per power | Requires at least one dot in the relevant Force Skill                |
| **Background**           | 1 point            | Raises a background rating by 1                                      |
| **Merit**                | 1 point per point  | Purchase merits at their listed point cost                           |
| **Force Points**         | 2 points           | Raises maximum Force Points by 1                                     |
| **Willpower**            | 2 points           | Raises maximum Willpower by 1                                        |
| **Dark Side Resistance** | 1 point            | Raises Dark Side Resistance by 1                                     |

### Spending Strategy

Merits cost Freebie Points equal to their listed value (a `+3` Merit costs 3 Freebie Points). **Flaws** grant additional Freebie Points equal to their listed value (a `-3` Flaw gives you 3 more Freebie Points to spend). The net total of Merits minus Flaws cannot exceed **7 points** at character creation.

See [Merits & Flaws](../merits-flaws.mdx) for the full catalog.

**Example:** _Jax_ spends his 15 Freebie Points: raises Dexterity from 4 to 5 (5 points), raises Blaster from 3 to 4 using the Practiced Merit (2 points — see Practiced in Merits & Flaws), raises Resources from 2 to 3 (1 point), and takes the Merit **Daredevil** (+3, costs 3 points). That's 5 + 2 + 1 + 3 = 11 points. He takes the Flaw **Soft-Hearted** (-1, gains 1 point) and **Enemy: Crimson Dawn** (-3, gains 3 points) for 4 additional points. Total spent: 11 + 4 = 15.
