## Step 8: Force Skills & Powers (Optional)

If your character is **Force-sensitive** (has the Force Sensitive Merit or equivalent), you may spend Freebie Points to purchase Force Skills and Powers.

- **Force Skills** cost 3 Freebie Points per dot.
- **Force Powers** cost 2 Freebie Points each (requires at least one dot in the relevant Force Skill).
- All five Force Skills start at **0**.

| Force Skill     | Governs                                                                   |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Control**     | Powers affecting your own body — healing, ignoring pain, physical prowess |
| **Dynamism**    | Powers affecting energy — containing blaster bolts, Sith lightning        |
| **Rapport**     | Powers affecting minds — suggestion, calming beasts, reading thoughts     |
| **Sense**       | Powers of perception — awareness, clairvoyance, probing memories          |
| **Telekinesis** | Powers of motion — moving objects, crushing, force throws                 |

See [Force](../force.mdx) for the full Force Powers list and rules.
