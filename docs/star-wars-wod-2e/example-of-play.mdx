# Example of Play

This walkthrough follows **Jax Vorn**, a Corellian smuggler, through a cantina confrontation that goes sideways. Every numbered step shows the dice in action — refer to the character sheet below to see his full stats.

## The Setup

Jax is lying low on Ord Mantell when an old debt catches up with him. A Trandoshan bounty hunter named **Korrsk** slides into the booth across from him.

> **Korrsk:** "Jabba sent me. You know why."

Jax's player decides to talk their way out. Time to roll.

---

## Scene 1: The Negotiation

### Step 1 — Form the Dice Pool

Jax tries to convince Korrsk that Jabba's bounty isn't worth the trouble. Jax is a smuggler, not a diplomat — he relies on underworld savvy, not charm. The GM calls for a **Charisma + Streetwise** roll at difficulty **6**.

Jax has **Charisma 2** and **Streetwise 2**, giving him a pool of **4 dice**.

### Step 2 — Roll and Evaluate

<TWWrapper>
    <InlineRoll notation={'4d10\u003e=6f=1@7,9,3,6'} variant="details" preroll />
</TWWrapper>

**Evaluation:** The `7`, `9`, and `6` are successes (3 total). No ones. Net result: **3 successes** — a strong success.

### Step 3 — The GM Narrates

> Jax leans forward, voice low. "You think Jabba remembers my name? He's got a hundred pilots. Tell him I'm dead. Tell him I'm not worth the fuel."
>
> Korrsk's jaw clicks. He doesn't draw — yet. "You've got until I finish my drink."

**3 successes** at difficulty 6 is a clear win. Korrsk isn't convinced, but he's hesitating. The GM awards a temporary reprieve — long enough for Jax to make a break for it.

---

## Scene 2: The Blasterfight

Jax bolts for the door. Korrsk draws. Combat begins.

### Step 4 — Initiative

The GM calls for initiative. Everyone rolls **Wits + Alertness** at difficulty 6.

Jax has **Wits 3 + Alertness 2 = 5 dice**.

<TWWrapper>
    <InlineRoll notation={'5d10\u003e=6f=1@8,3,6,9,2'} variant="details" preroll />
</TWWrapper>

**3 successes.** Korrsk rolls and gets 1 success. Jax acts first.

### Step 5 — Attack Roll

Jax spins and fires his hold-out blaster. The GM sets difficulty **6** (standard range, no cover).

Jax has **Dexterity 5 + Blaster 4 = 9 dice**. Korrsk declares a dodge: **Dexterity 3 + Dodge 1 = 4 dice**, difficulty 6.

Jax rolls first:

<TWWrapper>
    <InlineRoll notation={'9d10\u003e=6f=1@6,4,8,2,7,1,9,3,5'} variant="details" preroll />
</TWWrapper>

**Net: 4 successes** (five successes, one canceled by the `1`). Korrsk rolls to dodge:

<TWWrapper>
    <InlineRoll notation={'4d10\u003e=6f=1@3,7,2,1'} variant="details" preroll />
</TWWrapper>

**Net: 0 successes** (the `7` succeeds, the `1` cancels it). Jax's 4 successes easily beat the difficulty — **the bolt hits**.

### Step 6 — Damage Roll

A hold-out blaster deals **5 dice** of damage at difficulty 6.

<TWWrapper>
    <InlineRoll notation={'5d10\u003e=6@8,4,6,9,2'} variant="details" preroll />
</TWWrapper>

**3 successes.** Korrsk takes **3 levels of Lethal damage**.

### Step 7 — Soak Roll

As a named NPC, Korrsk gets a soak roll. He has **Stamina 4**.

<TWWrapper>
    <InlineRoll notation={'4d10\u003e=6@7,5,3,8'} variant="details" preroll />
</TWWrapper>

**2 successes.** Soak reduces the damage from 3 to **1 level** (the heroic soak rule: PCs and named NPCs always take at least 1 Lethal damage).

> Korrsk roars as the bolt catches him in the shoulder. He staggers but doesn't go down — and now he's angry.

### Step 8 — Wound Penalties

Korrsk has taken 1 Lethal wound (Hurt). He takes a **-1 dice penalty** on all future actions.

---

## Scene 3: The Escape

Jax dives behind the bar. There's a service door in the back — but it's locked with a security panel.

### Step 9 — Extended Action: Slicing the Panel

The GM calls for an **extended action**. Jax needs to accumulate **5 successes** on **Dexterity + Security** rolls (difficulty 6). Each roll represents one turn. A botch resets progress to zero.

Jax has **Dexterity 5 + Security 2 = 7 dice**.

**Turn 1:**

<TWWrapper>
    <InlineRoll notation={'7d10\u003e=6f=1@5,8,2,7,1,4,9'} variant="details" preroll />
</TWWrapper>

**Net: 2 successes** (three successes, one canceled by the `1`). Total: **2 / 5**.

**Turn 2:**

<TWWrapper>
    <InlineRoll notation={'7d10\u003e=6f=1@9,4,6,3,8,2,5'} variant="details" preroll />
</TWWrapper>

**3 successes.** Total: **5 / 5** — the door clicks open in just two turns. Jax's high Dexterity makes short work of the lock.

> Jax rips the panel cover off and shorts the lock. The door hisses open a crack. He's through it before Korrsk can get off another shot.

---

## Scene 4: The Chase

Jax runs through the back alleys of Ord Mantell. Korrsk follows, bleeding but relentless.

### Step 10 — Resisted Action: The Chase

The GM frames this as a **resisted extended action**. Both characters roll **Stamina** each turn (Jax has no Athletics dots, so he rolls raw Stamina). First to accumulate **10 net successes** wins.

Jax has **Stamina 3 = 3 dice**. Korrsk, wounded (-1 penalty), has **Stamina 4 + Athletics 1 - 1 = 4 dice**.

**Round 1 — Jax:**

<TWWrapper>
    <InlineRoll notation={'3d10\u003e=6f=1@8,3,6'} variant="details" preroll />
</TWWrapper>

**2 successes.** Korrsk:

<TWWrapper>
    <InlineRoll notation={'4d10\u003e=6f=1@7,2,4,6'} variant="details" preroll />
</TWWrapper>

**2 successes.** They cancel. No progress.

**Round 2 — Jax:**

<TWWrapper>
    <InlineRoll notation={'3d10\u003e=6f=1@10,7,5'} variant="details" preroll />
</TWWrapper>

**2 successes (10 explodes — roll again: `3`, no extra).** Korrsk:

<TWWrapper>
    <InlineRoll notation={'4d10\u003e=6f=1@2,5,9,1'} variant="details" preroll />
</TWWrapper>

**Net: 0 successes** (the `1` cancels the `9`). Jax gains **+2 net**. Running total: **2 / 10**.

> Jax vaults a stack of crates and doubles back toward the spaceport. Korrsk's wound is slowing him down.

**Round 3 — Jax:**

<TWWrapper>
    <InlineRoll notation={'3d10\u003e=6f=1@4,8,6'} variant="details" preroll />
</TWWrapper>

**2 successes.** Korrsk:

<TWWrapper>
    <InlineRoll notation={'4d10\u003e=6f=1@3,2,1,1'} variant="details" preroll />
</TWWrapper>

**Botch!** Two ones and no successes. Korrsk trips, losing ground. The GM rules this grants Jax an automatic **+5 successes** to his total. Running total: **9 / 10**.

**Round 4 — Jax:**

<TWWrapper>
    <InlineRoll notation={'3d10\u003e=6f=1@5,7,9'} variant="details" preroll />
</TWWrapper>

**2 successes.** Running total: **11 / 10**.

> Jax hears a crash behind him. He doesn't look back. He's at the docking bay. His ship, the _Stardust Drifter_, is right where he left it.

---

## Step 11 — Resolution

Jax fires up the engines and lifts off. Korrsk limps to the bay doors, firing blaster bolts at the ship's underside — but he's too late.

The GM awards **2 XP** for the session: 1 for survival, 1 for clever roleplaying (the failed negotiation that escalated into a fight, the creative escape route).
