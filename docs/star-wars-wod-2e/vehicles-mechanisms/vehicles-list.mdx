# Vehicle Catalog

Vehicles stat blocks converted from WEG source books. Each entry follows the conversion guidelines from [Vehicle Traits & Systems](./traits-systems.mdx).

The catalog covers ground vehicles, walkers, speeders, starfighters, space transports, and capital ships from the WEG core rulebook.
